Data Quality Checks

1. Columns should be lowercase with spaces are replaced by underscore
2. Check if datatype are changed properly
3. Check if nulls/blanks are handled properly


Analysis Questions 

% column is optional using that gives extra credit - as percentages are easy to understand than numbers for business people(If not used explain this)

1. Monthly Revenue Trend
Merge Sales, Products, Exchange Rates. Calculate total revenue USD by month (2024).
Output: Year | Month | Revenue_USD (12 rows)

2. Peak Month Analysis
From Q1, identify top 3 revenue months. Calculate % of annual total.
Output: Month | Revenue_USD | %of_Total

3. Holiday Drivers
For Q2 peak months, show top 3 product categories driving revenue.
Output: Category | Peak_Month_Revenue | %of_Peak_Total

4. Delivery Performance
Calculate overall avg delivery time across all orders.
Output: Average_Days | Total_Orders

5. Country Delivery Issues
Avg delivery time by store country. Show slowest 5 countries.
Output: Country | Avg_Days | Order_Count | Median_Days

6. Channel Performance
AOV (revenue/orders) by Online vs In-Store across continents (Online = StoreKey.isna()).
Output: Continent | AOV_Online | AOV_Store | Online_Orders | Store_Orders

7. Volume Leaders
Top 5 product categories by total units sold.
Output: Rank | Category | Units_Sold | %of_Total_Units

8. Revenue Leaders
Top 5 product categories by total revenue USD.
Output: Rank | Category | Revenue_USD | %of_Total_Revenue

9. Customer Profile
Customer count and spending by Continent × Gender.
Output: Continent | Gender | Customer_Count | Total_Spend_USD | Avg_Spend_Per_Cust

10. Customer Loyalty
Repeat customer rate (% with 2+ orders) by continent.
Output: Continent | Repeat_Rate_% | Unique_Customers | Repeat_Customers
